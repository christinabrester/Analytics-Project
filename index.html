Solar PV forecasting

  • Solar PV outcome
  • NWP data and power forecast
  • Modeling



Here is a collection of experimental results obtained in the joint project
by the Finnish Meteorological Institute and the University of Eastern Finland.
We introduce data-driven and physics-based forecasts of hourly solar PV outcome for the next 24 hours.


All the details might be found in the paper:
Brester et al., 2022. Evaluating neural network models in site-specific solar PV forecasting using numerical weather prediction data and weather observations
(submitted to Renew. Energy) and on GitHub: Analytics Project.
